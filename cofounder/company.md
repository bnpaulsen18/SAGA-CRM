## The problem
Nonprofits lose roughly **3 of every 4 first-time donors** within a year (sector first-year retention ≈ 20–25%). Winning a new donor costs **5–7× more** than keeping one. Legacy donor CRMs (Salesforce NPSP, Bloomerang, DonorPerfect, Blackbaud) are built for data entry and reporting, not for action — they're expensive, clunky, and mostly ignored by overstretched teams.

## The product (verified from demos + codebase)
- **Morning Brief** — SAGA reviews every donor overnight and surfaces the day's highest-impact actions, with outreach **drafted in the staff member's own voice**. Approve-and-send; nothing goes out without a human.
- **Retention / reactivation engine** — engagement scoring, lapse-risk flags ("cooling", "lapse risk", "champion"), and reactivation workflows. Product claim: SAGA cohorts retain first-year donors at ~41% vs ~24% sector. *Confirm with real customer data before using this externally.*
- **Direct payouts via Stripe Connect** — donations settle directly into the org's own Stripe account; SAGA applies a 2% platform fee per donation.
- **Campaigns, recurring/sustainer giving, donation pages, communications, onboarding wizard.**
- **Stack:** Next.js (App Router) · Prisma · Stripe (Connect) · Auth.
