## Narrative spine (10-slide arc)
1. **Hook** — Nonprofits lose ~75% of first-time donors within a year. That leaky bucket is the sector's biggest, most ignored problem.
2. **Problem** — Legacy donor CRMs (Salesforce NPSP, Bloomerang, DonorPerfect, Blackbaud) are systems of record built for data entry — expensive, clunky, and ignored by stretched teams.
3. **Why now** — AI finally makes per-donor personalization at scale possible. The old tools structurally can't do it.
4. **Solution** — SAGA, the AI-native donor CRM. The **Morning Brief** surfaces the day's highest-impact donor actions, drafted in your voice. Approve and send.
5. **How it works** — Overnight, SAGA reviews every donor → ranks actions by impact → drafts outreach → human approves. Engagement scoring, lapse-risk, reactivation workflows.
6. **Wedge & model** — the AI that does the busywork (the Morning Brief) is the adoption wedge; monetize via a **$100/mo subscription + a 2% platform fee** on donations (gifts settle straight to the org's Stripe).
