## Cofounder reality check — read before you pitch this number
$5M is large for a **pre-seed**. Typical pre-seed is ~$250k–$1.5M; $5M is seed-sized. That is not a "no" — it's a calibration. To credibly raise $5M this early you need at least one of:
1. **Standout team pedigree** (repeat founder, notable operator, domain authority), or
2. **Real early traction** (revenue, retention proof, marquee logos), or
3. **A specific, defensible reason the wedge needs that much capital now.**

If none is clearly true yet, the cleaner paths are: (a) frame this as a **seed** so investor expectations match the number, or (b) raise a smaller pre-seed now and a priced seed once traction is in. Decide this deliberately — don't let the number be an accident.
